Dino Game

The Chrome Dino game, for your calculator

Features

  • Includes the Cacti and Birds
  • Player + Birds are animated
  • Keeps track of your Highscore
  • Gets faster as you progress through the game

Idea

This game is simply meant as a nice and small game that is playable on your calculator (CASIO fx-cg50). It's also not supposed to be a really big or especially exciting game, just something small you can play when you are bored and don't know what to do.

Strucure

  • Written entirely in C
  • Uses the Prizm-SDK
  • Uses a modified version of my Prizm-Engine
